About the ACSL Race League

The ACSL Race League is a competitive and community-focused sim racing championship built around Assetto Corsa. Our goal is to provide a structured, fair and enjoyable racing environment for both new and experienced drivers. ACSL combines clean racing, clear communication and a transparent points system to create a league that is accessible yet challenging.

What We Offer

ACSL hosts a full racing season featuring a variety of tracks, special events and community-driven ideas. Drivers can follow their progress through our standings system, explore the race calendar, read league articles and stay updated with announcements. Every part of the league is designed to be easy to navigate and informative.

Our Philosophy

We believe in clean racing, respect between drivers and a welcoming environment for all participants. ACSL encourages improvement, consistency and sportsmanship. Whether you are here to compete for the championship or simply enjoy organized racing, the league provides a place where everyone can grow.

Technology and Platform

The ACSL Race League runs on Assetto Corsa and uses custom tools to manage standings, events and communication. This website is built with lightweight HTML, CSS and JavaScript to ensure fast loading times and accessibility on all devices.
